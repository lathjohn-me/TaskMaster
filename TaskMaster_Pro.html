TaskMaster Pro

Connect Google Drive to sync tasks across all your devices. One-time setup — all in your browser.

1
Go to console.cloud.google.com and sign in with your personal Google account.
2
Click Select a project → New Project. Name it TaskMaster and click Create.
3
Search Google Drive API and click Enable.
4
Go to Credentials → Create Credentials → OAuth client ID. Choose Web application. Add https://lathjohn-me.github.io as Authorised JavaScript origin. Copy the Client ID.
5
Go to OAuth consent screen → Audience and add your Gmail as a test user.

Or skip for now — tasks save locally only.

Add Task from Claude — Voice / Mobile

How to add tasks by voice on mobile: Open the Claude app, tap the mic and say something like:
"Add a task: Pay electricity bill, due 30 April, high priority, Finance category, remind me 1 day before"

Claude will reply with a tap-to-open link that pre-fills the task form. Just tap Save.
